About Apartament Zorilor Cactus

Zorilor Cactus is for us, the chance to be closer to you, our friends.

With a modern design, this apartment welcomes clients with state-of-the-art equipment such as flat screen TV with cable-digital channels, WIFI internet access, fully equipped kitchen with crockery and refrigerator, coffee maker, washing machine.

The wonderful view will delight any tourist, and its terrace will be a place you probably won't forget too soon.

This apartment is located on Strada Padurii (in the southern part of Cluj), in a particularly quiet area.
